Standard RL models struggle with "delayed regret signal" in the music recommendation: when a user interacts with an item but later triggers an "unlike" or "undislike" signal. For such events, we introduce a fine-grained retrospective mechanism to correct the credit assignment. 
For the immediate reward rt, we set it as a function for "user feedback type":
rt=ПүlistenвӢ…played_ratio+ПүlikeвӢ…Iyt=likeвҲ’ПүdislikeвӢ…Iyt=dislike,
